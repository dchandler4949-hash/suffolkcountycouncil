Don’t usually vote? You can still make a difference.

This page is for people in Mildenhall High Town, Barton Mills Parish, Worlington Parish, and Freckenham Parish who often don’t vote — but want things to improve locally.

Your voice matters more than you think

Local elections often have low turnout. That means a small number of votes can decide what happens next. If you’ve felt ignored or let down before, I want to hear what would change that.

Message me with 3 quick points

  1. Which area you live in (Mildenhall / Barton Mills / Worlington / Freckenham)
  2. The one issue you want fixed first
  3. What’s stopped you voting before (optional)
